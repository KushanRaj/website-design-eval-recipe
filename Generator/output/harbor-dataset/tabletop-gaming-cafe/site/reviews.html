Heat: Pedal to the Metal — Modular Heat goes the distance

Reviewed by Mae Hellerud · with Devon & Ren · 12 min read

Heat earned its reputation in a single weekend at the cafe — five strangers ended Saturday yelling "ONE MORE LAP" at the bar. The base game alone is excellent; the modules turn it into the deepest racing experience on the shelf.

What makes it sing: the gear-shift hand management is dead simple to teach, but the corner-cornering punishes optimism just enough. Add the Garage and the legacy-light championship and you've got a five-month group ritual.
