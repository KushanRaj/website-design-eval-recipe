Booking FAQ

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 48 hours before your start time. Inside 48 hours we keep the deposit, but you can move the reservation once for free.

Can we bring outside food or drink?

Outside drinks no — we're a licensed bar. Outside food yes for birthdays/special occasions if you flag it ahead, $15 cake plating fee covers utensils and cleanup.

Are kids welcome?

Of course. Until 8pm any room is family-time; after 8pm The Hex Hall and Dragon's Den shift to 16+ unless you've reserved the room privately. Quiet Corner is always kid-friendly.

Do you teach the games?

Yes — add the Dedicated Host upgrade and we'll teach up to three titles in your session. Otherwise our floor staff will gladly help when they're free, and the librarian writes a one-page cheat sheet for every game.

What about accessibility?

Step-free entrance off the parking lot, accessible restroom on the main floor, large-print rules cards available on request, and the Quiet Corner has dimmable lighting for sensory-sensitive guests.
