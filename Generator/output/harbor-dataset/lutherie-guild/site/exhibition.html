The Annual Exhibition

Held each year in a chosen Hall, the Annual Exhibition gathers the year’s instruments before a jury of five sitting masters.

MMXXVI
Cremona · Palazzo dell’Arte
Plate I · Best in Show

The ‘Cygnet’ Violin

Atelier Beaufort & Cie. · Mirecourt

Built upon a Strad-pattern long-body in Bosnian maple and Val di Fiemme spruce, with a private varnish of cooked propolis, larch and madder.

“The jury was struck, at once, by an even and forgiving G-string, a singing C above the staff, and a quiet authority across the bow’s travel. Workmanship of the highest order.”

— The Mastership Jury, MMXXVI
