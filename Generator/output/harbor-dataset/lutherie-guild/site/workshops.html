Cap. II

Of the Member Workshops

A directory of the one hundred and forty benches now bound to the Charter, each with its house mark and its long account.

Each workshop holds full Charter Standing and may receive apprentices subject to the conditions laid in Cap. III. House marks are struck on the inner label of every instrument leaving the bench.

To Become a Member Workshop

Petitions are received in the spring and autumn quarters. The Charter requires a minimum of seven years at the bench under a recognised master, three instruments submitted blind for jury, and the testimony of two existing members.

Application Steps
