Retreat application Six questions Replied within fourteen days

One of six — slowly.

Which retreat are you applying for?

If unsure, name the date range you have in mind. We will write back with suggestions.

Two of six.

What is your name, and how shall we reach you?

Three of six.

Briefly, what is the shape of your practice so far?

Three or four sentences is plenty. There is no right answer; we read each application by hand.

Four of six.

Have you sat through at least one half-day, anywhere?

We ask this only of those signing on for residential retreats. If not yet, we recommend our Saturday open zazen first.

Five of six.

Health, allergies, anything we should know.

Held in confidence. Includes back injuries, dietary needs, medication schedules, recent surgeries.

Six of six. Last one.

Dana — what feels right, and what is workable.

The suggested band is a guide, not a price. If full dana is a barrier, please tell us; the scholarship fund exists for this.

We read each application by hand. Replies arrive within fourteen days; sometimes by post, always with care. If you have not heard from us in three weeks, please write again — letters do go astray.
