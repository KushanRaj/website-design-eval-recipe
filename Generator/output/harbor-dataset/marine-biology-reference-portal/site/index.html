Chondrichthyes FEATURED SPECIES
Whale Shark
Rhincodon typus
Smith, 1828
EN — Endangered ORDER:Orectolobiformes Epipelagic Coastal

The world's largest fish, reaching up to 18.8 m in length. A filter feeder found in warm, tropical ocean waters worldwide. Listed as Endangered on the IUCN Red List due to fishing, vessel strikes, and habitat degradation.
