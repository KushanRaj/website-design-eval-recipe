Security Note · Week of May 19

"The 'lost-package' text scam — and the one question that ends it."

By Avis Cardenas · Fraud & Member Protection

This week we've seen a sharp uptick in members reporting SMS messages claiming a package is "undeliverable" and prompting them to click a link to pay a small re-delivery fee. The links route to fake credit-card harvesting pages.

The single question that ends the scam: "Which carrier shipped it, and what was the tracking number?" A real carrier will know, instantly, both. A scammer will pivot or vanish.

If you have already clicked, call our 24/7 fraud hotline immediately. We can freeze and reissue your card from your couch — no branch visit required.
