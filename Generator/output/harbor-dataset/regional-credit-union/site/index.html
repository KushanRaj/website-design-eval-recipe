Member Voices · No. 47
I joined Three Rivers in 1981, two months after the mill closed. My uncle told me, plainly, that the credit union was the only place in town that would call you a member instead of a customer — and the only place where the person making the loan decision still lived on your road.

Forty-four years later, my granddaughter just opened her first share account at the very same branch in Riverbend. The pen on the counter is, I swear to you, the same pen.

— Margaret Holcomb · Member since 1981 · Riverbend Branch
