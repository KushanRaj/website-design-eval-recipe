Assumptions & disclosures

All figures are estimates. Rates shown are illustrative APRs effective the date of last publication, not a loan offer. Real underwriting depends on credit history, income, collateral, and a half-hour conversation with a member-services representative.

  • Mortgage PMI assumes single-family principal residence above 80% LTV.
  • Auto calculator assumes simple interest and no prepayment penalty.
  • Amortization compounds monthly; the first payment is dated 30 days after closing.
  • HELOCs are variable-rate products tied to Prime; expect quarterly adjustments.
