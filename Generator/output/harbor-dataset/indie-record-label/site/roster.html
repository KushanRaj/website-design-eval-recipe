Demos & submissions

> We open the demo inbox twice a year: April 1–30 and October 1–31.
> Send one (1) link, a one-paragraph bio, and the city you play out of.
> If we don't write back inside six weeks, assume we listened and moved on.
> We do not return cassettes. We do read every email.
> tape@xeroxpressing.co
