Liner Notes

Riso Twins are not twins. They are J. Marek and L. Konvalinka, two people who met in 2019 at a riso-print zine fair in Greenpoint and proceeded to spend the next four years making music in a one-bedroom apartment above a laundromat. Pink Static is the record they have been quietly building the whole time.

The album was recorded between February and August 2023 in two rooms: the laundromat apartment and the back room of the Brooklyn shop. It was mastered loud, pressed louder, and is intended to be played on a system that can move air. Headphones will not do. We tested.

The title track Pink Static began as a four-bar loop on a borrowed Casio. The album closes with the same loop, slowed down and run through a tape delay that was almost certainly not working correctly. The thing in between is six tracks of pink-saturated machine music that we are very proud to put on a record.

Thank you to: M. Stamp for the sleeves, R. Salinas for the patience, the laundromat downstairs for the white noise, and every person who pre-ordered without hearing a note.

— J.M. & L.K., Brooklyn, March 2024.
