We press our own records.

Twelve years, forty-seven catalog numbers, two basements, one Heidelberg, and a stack of artists who refuse to be quiet. This is the dispatch from Xerox Pressing Co., a record label between Brooklyn and Glasgow that still smells like ink and solder.
