Sold-out "Into the Woods" closes Riverbend's largest theatre season ever

By Anh Tran · May 16, 2026

The Riverbend High Theatre Guild brought down the house with six packed performances of Sondheim's Into the Woods — a production that crossed the stage with three years' worth of growing ambition.

"You could feel the parent guild's stitches in every costume — and the audience's roar when Cinderella finally found her voice."

Director Mr. Park said the show was a milestone after a half-decade of rebuilding the program. Sixty-eight cast and crew students participated, with set pieces designed by the CTE woodshop.
