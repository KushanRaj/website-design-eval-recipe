Academics

A curious mind for every age

Our curriculum mixes the basics (we love a good multiplication fact) with project-based learning, world languages from kindergarten, and college and career pathways at the high school.

Subjects — Early Learning

📚 English Language Arts
Phonics-rich, knowledge-building literacy with daily read-alouds, decodable readers, and writers' workshop. We use the EL Education and Wit & Wisdom curricula in K-5.
🔢 Mathematics
Conceptual and procedural fluency through Illustrative Mathematics. We assess every six weeks with low-stakes checks and growth-tracking.
🔬 Science
NGSS-aligned units rooted in phenomena from the river valley — wetlands, weather, motion. STEAM Lab time in grades 3-8.
🌍 Social Studies
Local-first social studies grounded in the Anishinaabe, settler, and immigrant histories of the valley, with civics in grades 5, 8, and 12.
🗣️ World Languages
Spanish from kindergarten at all elementaries. Middle school adds Mandarin, French, and ASL. High school offers six languages and dual-credit options.
🎨 Arts
Weekly visual art, vocal music, and instrumental music starting in grade 4. Band, choir, orchestra, theatre, and ceramics at the high school.
🏃 PE & Health
Daily movement K-5, daily PE block 6-8, and four required PE/health credits in high school. SHAPE national standards.
🛠️ CTE
Career & Technical Education pathways in welding, health science, computer science, culinary, and education.

Program Spotlights

Dual Language Spanish

K-5 immersion at Birchwood Elementary. 90% Spanish in K, gradually balanced to 50/50 by grade 5.

STEAM Lab

Maker-space residencies at Hollow Creek and MH Middle — robotics, 3D printing, and bridge-building.

AVID

College-readiness program in grades 7-12 for students who'll be first in their family to attend college.

IB Diploma Programme

Riverbend HS is an authorized IB World School with full diploma and course-by-course candidates.

CTE Pathways

Earn industry-recognized credentials in welding, health, coding, culinary, or education before graduation.

Graduation Pathways

Three ways to walk the stage

A

Traditional Diploma

24 credits across required courses and electives — the Maple Hollow default.

B

IB Diploma

Six IB subjects, Theory of Knowledge, an Extended Essay, and CAS hours.

C

CTE Pathway Diploma

Traditional credits plus a sequenced CTE concentration and capstone portfolio.
