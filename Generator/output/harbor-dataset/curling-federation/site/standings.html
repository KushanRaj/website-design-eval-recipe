Scoring

Each Premier Division match awards 8 pts for a win, 4 pts for an extra-end loss, and 0 pts for a regulation loss. Tiebreaks resolve via DSC and head-to-head record per §4.7 of the handbook.

Notes

Standings frozen at the conclusion of round 22. Playoff seeding is reflected on the tournament bracket. Withdrawals forfeit accrued points per §2.3.
