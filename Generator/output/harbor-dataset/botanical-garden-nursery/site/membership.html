Patronage · Members & Conservation

Become a Member

The conservatory is a public trust held together by patrons. Members underwrite our propagation programme, conservation work in the Eastern Himalaya, and the keeping-open of the gates.

I joined Fernwood the year my son was born; he is now thirty-eight and still won't visit a garden unless they shelve the labels by family. The garden, somehow, kept us both reading.
— Eleanor Riordan · Patron since 1987 · Quilcene

Across four generations our members have funded the propagation yard, the conservation surveys in Arunachal Pradesh, the orchid bench, the rebuilt Camellia Glade, the small library of botanical folios open to all on Sundays, and very nearly every plant you can read about in the Encyclopedia.

IV.

Frequent Questions

Answered by the membership office
What does my membership get me at the gate?

Show your card (or our app, or a friendly face — staff are good with names). You may bring guests up to your tier's pass count, free of charge, every visit, every day. We do not require advance booking for members.

How does the reciprocal garden programme work?

The Household, Patron, and Curator's Circle tiers include membership in the American Horticultural Society Reciprocal Admissions Program — admitting you free to 327 partner gardens across North America. Bring your card and a printed ID; some gardens limit parking discounts to home members.

Is my membership tax-deductible?

Fernwood Conservatory is a registered 501(c)(3) garden trust. Most of your membership above the fair-market value of benefits is tax-deductible in the United States. A receipt issues automatically by email; we are happy to prepare an itemised statement on request.

Can I change tiers mid-year?

Yes. Step up at any time and pay only the prorated difference. Step down at renewal — we do not refund mid-year, but we will credit unused workshop discounts to your next session.

Can I leave a bequest or endowed seat?

We are delighted to discuss legacy giving, named benches, sponsored cultivars, and endowed glasshouse panels. Write to legacy@fernwood.garden or call our office at +1 (360) 555 0124.

Do you offer member-only events?

Yes. Six per year, ranging from the spring open-flask evening to the November bookbinding seminar with our director. Plus, member-preview Tuesdays before every workshop, and quarterly behind-the-bench tours.
