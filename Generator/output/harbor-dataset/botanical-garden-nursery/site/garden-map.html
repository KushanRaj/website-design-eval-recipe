The Glasshouses

Three connected Victorian-style glasshouses housing the orchid, fernery, and tropical collections. Maintained at 18–24 °C with diffused light through frosted panes.

Signature collection
Paphiopedilum, Platycerium, Vireya rhododendrons
Accessibility
Step-free entry · Glasshouse I has a tactile orchid bench
