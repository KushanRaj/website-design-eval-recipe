Questions, frequently asked

FAQ

Is Frequency Room actually independent?

Yes. We are an owner-operated audio co-op of twelve resident shows. We have never sold equity, and the only outside money we accept is host-read sponsorship and member dues.

What happens to my feeds if I cancel?

Your ad-free feeds and bonus episodes stay accessible through the end of your billing period, then your feeds revert to standard. You keep your saved archive searches forever, even on the free tier.

Can I gift a membership?

Yes, both tiers. A gifted Skeleton Key ships the print zine to the recipient's address for the duration of the gift.

Is there a student or fixed-income rate?

Yes. Write to circle@frequencyroom.fm from any .edu address or a brief note about your situation and we'll send you a discount link. No documentation needed.

Will my contribution support every show on the network?

Yes. Member dues are pooled, split evenly across the resident shows by airtime each quarter, and a small percentage is reserved for new-show development and infrastructure.

Can I switch tiers later?

Whenever you like. We prorate the difference for the current period. No upsell emails — we promise.
