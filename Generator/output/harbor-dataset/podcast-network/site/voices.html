Voice Feature · CH 03 · The Late Signal

Mara
Quan

Mara Quan grew up between Kuala Lumpur and Vancouver, in two cities that, in her telling, "both rained in different keys." She joined Frequency Room in 2024 from The Brooklyn Witness, where she had spent six years as the paper's classical music critic and had personally rejected seventy-one press releases for solo piano recitals.

She co-hosts The Late Signal with August Vellor, takes most interviews barefoot, and keeps a personal index card on every guest the show has ever had. The card is filed by the room they recorded in, not by name. She has explained this once and refuses to explain it again.

Career timeline

2011Music staff, The McGill Daily, Montreal.
2014Classical desk freelancer, The Globe and Mail.
2018Full-time classical critic, The Brooklyn Witness.
2022Guest essayist, NPR Music; first short-form audio.
2024Joins Frequency Room. Co-launches The Late Signal.
2025Webby honoree, "A Greenhouse for One Singer."
