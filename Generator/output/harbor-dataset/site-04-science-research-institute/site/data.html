Access guidance

How to cite, reuse, and integrate

Licensing. Most Meridian datasets are released under CC-BY-4.0. Imagery products carry CC-BY-NC-4.0 where required by partner agreements. All datasets are DOI-assigned at first public release.

Citing. Every dataset card has a "Cite (DOI)" action that returns BibTeX, RIS, and CSL-JSON. When citing in a publication, please include the DOI in your data availability statement; we monitor reuse via DataCite.

Programmatic access. All datasets are exposed through an OpenAPI v3 endpoint at api.meridian-obs.org/v3. ERDDAP and THREDDS catalogs mirror most gridded and time-series products. Python and R clients are maintained by the Data Office.

Quality flags. Real-time streams are flagged provisional and reach final quality after the next service cruise. Final products include per-sample QARTOD flags.
