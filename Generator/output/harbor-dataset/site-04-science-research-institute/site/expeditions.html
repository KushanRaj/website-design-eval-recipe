MDSO-24-B · Gulf of Maine Benthic Survey

R/V Bartholomew Strait · Leg B · 10 – 24 May 2026 · Day 9 of 14 · 41.8°N 67.6°W

This leg continues a 14-day program of multibeam mapping and ROV photogrammetry across the Wilkinson and Jordan Basins, with priority sponge-garden stations on the Murray Basin slope. Daily multibeam coverage targets 18 km² in good weather.

Day 1 · 10 MayDay 9 · 18 May (today)Day 14 · 24 May
Daily Log

Field dispatches from the chief scientist

Day 9 · 18 May · 06:42 UTC
The deep chorus is strangely quiet tonight

Two hours of clean hydrophone time over the eastern Murray slope. We finished a clean multibeam swath at 41.78°N. Tomorrow: ROV dive on station MB-04 if winds drop below 18 kt.

Day 8 · 17 May
Solstice recovered, port photogrammetry sled deployed

Solstice surfaced on schedule with all 26 missions intact. Heading: Jordan Basin slope transect 4.

Day 7 · 16 May
Storm shadow, productive day on the rosette

30 CTD casts to 240 m. Visiting student F. Quintero ran her first uninterrupted set of duplicates — congratulations.

Day 6 · 15 May
Weather hold lifted; transit completed

Wave height down to 1.4 m. Off to station MB-02.
